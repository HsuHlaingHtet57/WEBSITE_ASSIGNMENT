Privacy Policy

At StarLight, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide when using our website and services.

This information is used solely for processing orders, improving our services, and communicating with you effectively.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. However, trusted service providers may assist us with operations such as payment processing and delivery, and they are obligated to protect your data under strict confidentiality agreements.

By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy. We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally, and any changes will be reflected on this page. If you have questions about your data or how we use it, please contact us directly.
